Organic Food

Farm fresh ingredients. Delivered to your door.

Description

Organic Food is a US startup which creates delicious and nourishing smoothies, soups, oats, lattes and more. Delivered at home, by a system of weekly boxes. They worked with Work & Co to rebuild their mobile app, which allows users to compose their boxes for the following weeks.
